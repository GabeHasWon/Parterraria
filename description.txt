----- Parterraria! -----
by GabeHasWon
commissioned & concepted by Megaswave

Parterraria! is a mod that adds in the ability to play Mario Party-styled games within Terraria.
You can build the boards, build the minigames, and play with any amount of friends!

The mod is fairly complex as it has a ton of moving parts, so there will be additional description attached later.

(beta 1)
