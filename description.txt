----- Parterraria! -----
by GabeHasWon
commissioned & concepted by Megaswave

Parterraria! is a mod that adds in the ability to play Mario Party-styled games within Terraria.
You can build the boards, build the minigames, and play with any amount of friends!

Collect Amethyst Coins and Celestial Cores, play hectic multiplayer games and roll to win in
Parterraria!

----
Full feature list:

CUSTOMIZABLE BOARDS: 
	Create boards by connecting "nodes" (spaces) to each other to create any board you want
	Configure the boards to modify how many coins you get, how expensive stuff is, how long the turns are, and more
	Create as many boards as you can fit in a world, and play any at any time

SYNCHRONOUS BOARDPLAY
	Roll and explore your boards with your friends at all once - no turns
	Plan routes and paths to get the best luck you can

BUILDABLE MINIGAMES:
	Build areas for minigames to be played in to add constant variety to your games
	Use wiring or other mods to vary these even further
	Configure the minigames to your specific liking, changing item spawn locations or the size of the playable area

COMPLETE MULTIPLAYER SUPPORT
	Build and play boards with friends
	Play with as many players as you can get to join you on a board at once
